WEB DEVELOPER

A web developer is a professional responsible for designing, building, and maintaining websites. They work with various programming languages, such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, to create the visual layout and interactive features of a site.
Web developers collaborate with designers to bring their vision to life and ensure the functionality of the website meets both user and business requirements.
They also troubleshoot issues, optimize performance, and stay updated on the latest web development trends and technologies.
